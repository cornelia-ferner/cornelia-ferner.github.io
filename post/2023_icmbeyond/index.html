Workshop at ICMbeyond 2023

Feb 1, 2023

The conference Inverted Classroom and beyond has become an indispensable stimulus for the further development of the inverted classroom model and innovative didactics in the German-speaking world. It will take place on February 16 and 17, 2023 in Chur, Switzerland.

At the conference, I will offer a workshop on “possibilities and limits for using chatbots in teaching” together with my colleague Eva Lienbacher. This workshop is a transfer measure from the project “Schnatterbüchse - Chatbots in der Lehre” and is intended to generate feedback on the format on the one hand, and to capture opinions on the use of chatbots for teaching on the other.
