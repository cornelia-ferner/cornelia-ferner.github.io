Research Project “Chatbots for Higher Education” Starts

In March 2022, the interdisciplinary research project “Chatbots for Higher Education” starts, in which I and Prof. Dr. Eva Lienbacher (Business degree program) are exploring ways of applying chatbots for educational purposes. Chatbots can be applied to support with different teaching tasks, such as assisting with FAQs, mentoring or the simulation of expert interviews in professional settings, where the chatbot plays the role of customers, patients etc.

During a two year period, two proof-of-concepts and a use case study will be developed and implemented together with our students. The target audience primarily are students from four of our degree programs in engineering and business and social sciences. In transfer workshops, we will share our knowledge with a broader audience. The findings will also help in establishing new academic partnerhsips and acquire funding for new research projects.

The project is funded by SUAS’s “Zukunftsfonds”.
